The NCUA released its first quarter 2026 state-level credit union data, showing federally insured credit unions' assets grew 2.8 percent at the median year-over-year while loans outstanding increased just 0.6 percent at the median. The report notes 85 percent of federally insured credit unions had positive year-to-date net income, with a median loan-to-share ratio of 68 percent nationally.
